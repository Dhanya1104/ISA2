Welcome to the Otter World

Otters are playful and intelligent mammals known for their love of water and their cute, mischievous personalities. They are incredibly social and use their strong bonds with family members to thrive in the wild. Otters are often seen swimming, playing, or even using tools to break open food.

Meet the Otters

  • Sea Otter: Found along the coasts of the Pacific Ocean, these otters are known for their dense fur and ability to use tools.
  • River Otter: These otters are excellent swimmers and thrive in freshwater rivers, streams, and lakes.
  • Eurasian Otter: Native to Europe and Asia, they are known for their elusive and solitary nature.
